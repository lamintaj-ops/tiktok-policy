About The Application

Columbia Pictures Aquaverse is a web-based social media management platform designed to streamline content operations. Our tool allows authorized users to manage their video content efficiently while maintaining full control over their account security.

How It Works with TikTok

Secure Authentication (OAuth 2.0)
Users voluntarily connect their TikTok accounts using the official TikTok Login (OAuth 2.0). After successful authorization, the app receives access tokens strictly with user-approved scopes.

Content Publishing
The app enables users to upload video content, add captions, and publish videos to their own TikTok accounts. Publishing occurs only through explicit user action or scheduled publishing initiated by the user via the Content Posting API.

Data Privacy & User Control

  • No Unauthorized Posting: The app does not post content without user authorization.
  • Limited Access: We do not access private data beyond the scopes explicitly approved by the user.
  • Full Control: Users can disconnect their TikTok account at any time. This action immediately revokes access and deletes stored tokens from our system.
